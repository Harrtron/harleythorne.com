Nudge

A gentle app for couples who want to feel more connected

Early stage Relationship design ADHD-friendly

Overview

Nudge is a gentle app for couples who want to feel more connected while getting everyday life done.

Instead of nagging or pressure, it turns small actions into moments of connection.

How It Works

  • ●One partner adds simple tasks ("Things") they'd like help with.
  • ●The other completes them in a satisfying, ADHD-friendly way - scribbling them out, one at a time.
  • ●When effort happens, it's visible and can be appreciated.

Moments

  • ●Gentle prompts for kindness or connection.
  • ●Small nudges like a hug, a smile, or a few minutes together.
  • ●Not chores - just reminders that relationships are built in the in-between.

Why It's Different

  • ●No streaks, scores, guilt, or dashboards.
  • ●Built around a simple idea: when effort feels good and gets noticed, people show up naturally.
  • ●Especially helpful when ADHD, overwhelm, or busy lives make communication harder.

What It Helps With

  • ●Reducing nagging and tension.
  • ●Making effort visible without arguments.
  • ●Helping partners feel appreciated.
  • ●Turning small wins into positive momentum.
  • ●Building connection in a busy world.

Who It's For

  • ●Couples who love each other but get stuck on the small stuff.
  • ●People who want things to feel lighter, not heavier.
  • ●Especially when one partner struggles more with initiation or organisation.

The Idea Behind It

  • ●Crossing something off a list together feels better than talking about why it didn't get done.
  • ●Nudge replaces pressure with progress - and progress with connection.
