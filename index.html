About

I build things from zero.

I'm at my best in messy, early-stage environments where the problem isn't fully defined, the resources are limited, and progress matters more than perfection. I move quickly, ship early, learn from real users, and keep momentum high.

I co-founded FanHub, a sports loyalty platform for football fans. We took it from idea to 50k+ monthly active users, over 200k registered fans, and $3m+ in venture funding. Along the way I owned whatever mattered most at the time — product, growth, commercial strategy, operations — and helped turn an idea into something people actually used, loved, and paid for.

What that taught me is simple: building something new isn't about titles or frameworks. It's about judgement, pace, and follow-through. Making decisions with incomplete information. Creating clarity where there isn't any. Setting standards, building systems, and helping teams ship under pressure.

I'm drawn to early-stage products, fast-moving teams, and work where you're creating something that didn't exist yesterday.
